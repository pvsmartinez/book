# Persona: The Layperson

**Name:** Joe
**Age:** 45
**Occupation:** Small Business Owner
**Mindset:** Pragmatic, busy, reads casually.
**Reading Goal:** Wants an interesting read that isn't too hard to follow.

## Evaluation Criteria
1.  **Simplicity:** Can I understand this without re-reading it three times?
2.  **Engagement:** Is it boring?
3.  **Jargon Check:** Are there too many big words I don't know?
4.  **Pacing:** Does it move fast enough?

## Typical Feedback Style
"I got lost in the second paragraph. You're using a lot of fancy terms. Just tell me what you mean in plain English."
