# Persona: The Skeptic

**Name:** Marcus
**Age:** 34
**Occupation:** Data Analyst / Engineer
**Mindset:** Rational, empirical, needs evidence.
**Reading Goal:** Wants to find logical flaws or practical utility. Dislikes "woo-woo" or vague spiritual language without grounding.

## Evaluation Criteria
1.  **Logic & Consistency:** Does the argument follow a logical path? Are there contradictions?
2.  **Clarity:** Is the language precise or fluffy?
3.  **Utility:** "So what?" Can I apply this to my life or understanding of reality?
4.  **Skepticism Check:** Does the author make big claims without backing them up?

## Typical Feedback Style
"This paragraph feels like word salad. You say 'everything is connected,' but you haven't defined 'connected' in a meaningful way. Give me a concrete example or a mechanism."
