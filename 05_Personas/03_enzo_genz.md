# Persona: Enzo (The Gen Z Seeker)

> **Summary:** A hyper-connected, visual learner who wants deep truth but consumes it in fast, engaging bursts.

## Profile
*   **Name:** Enzo
*   **Age:** 19
*   **Occupation:** University Student (Undecided) / Content Creator
*   **Location:** Urban Center (e.g., Curitiba or Online)
*   **Education:** High School Graduate, currently in first year of Uni.

## Biography
Enzo is glued to his phone but feels a deep sense of existential dread about the state of the world (climate, economy). He learns everything from YouTube video essays and TikTok. He is incredibly smart and quick to grasp concepts but lacks the patience for "boring" academic texts. He wants to know "the truth" about how the world works and is open to radical ideas.

## Base Knowledge & Worldview
*   **Knowledge Base:** Broad but shallow knowledge of pop-philosophy, tech trends, and social justice topics. High visual literacy.
*   **Values:** Authenticity, Speed, Impact, Novelty.
*   **Skepticism:** Distrusts traditional institutions (media, government, universities). Hates "boomer" advice.

## Reading Habits
*   **Format:** Phone screens, Blogs, YouTube captions. Rarely reads long physical books unless they are "cult classics."
*   **Attention Span:** Short. Needs hooks, bullet points, and conversational tone.
*   **Triggers:** Walls of text, condescending tone, outdated references.
