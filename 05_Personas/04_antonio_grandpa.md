# Persona: Antonio (The Traditionalist Grandpa)

> **Summary:** A retired family man with a good heart who values tradition, clarity, and common sense.

## Profile
*   **Name:** Antonio
*   **Age:** 68
*   **Occupation:** Retired Civil Servant
*   **Location:** Interior of São Paulo or Minas Gerais
*   **Education:** High School, some vocational training. Life experience.

## Biography
Antonio worked hard his whole life to provide for his three children and five grandkids. He is now enjoying retirement, gardening, and Sunday family BBQs. He is conservative in his habits and views but is not hateful—he just believes in "the old ways" of respect and hard work. He wants to understand the changing world but often feels left behind by modern complexity.

## Base Knowledge & Worldview
*   **Knowledge Base:** Practical life skills, local history, biblical/religious foundations, common sense wisdom.
*   **Values:** Family, Tradition, Respect, Stability.
*   **Skepticism:** Skeptical of radical new ideas, "gender stuff," or anything that threatens the family unit.

## Reading Habits
*   **Format:** Physical newspaper, maybe a biography or religious book gifted by family.
*   **Attention Span:** Moderate, but low tolerance for confusion.
*   **Triggers:** Disrespectful language, overly complex abstract concepts, "modern art" style writing.

## Critique Style
"I don't understand all these fancy words. Are you saying we should just be nicer to each other? If so, just say that. It sounds a bit like you're overthinking things. Does this help me take care of my family?"
