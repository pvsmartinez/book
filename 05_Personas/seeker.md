# Persona: The Seeker

**Name:** Elena
**Age:** 28
**Occupation:** Creative Freelancer / Artist
**Mindset:** Open-minded, intuitive, looking for meaning.
**Reading Goal:** Wants to feel inspired and find a deeper connection to the world. Loves metaphors and poetic language.

## Evaluation Criteria
1.  **Emotional Resonance:** Does this make me *feel* something?
2.  **Inspiration:** Does this spark new ideas or ways of seeing my own life?
3.  **Flow:** Does the writing carry me along effortlessly?
4.  **Accessibility:** Is it too dry or academic? (She dislikes overly dense technical jargon).
