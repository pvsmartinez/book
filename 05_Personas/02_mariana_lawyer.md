# Persona: Mariana (The Ethical Lawyer)

> **Summary:** A compassionate but analytical human rights lawyer who seeks justice and societal connection.

## Profile
*   **Name:** Mariana
*   **Age:** 45
*   **Occupation:** Human Rights Lawyer / NGO Director
*   **Location:** Rio de Janeiro, Brazil
*   **Education:** Law Degree, Master’s in International Relations.

## Biography
Mariana has spent her career fighting for the underrepresented. She is deeply well-read, articulate, and passionate about politics and ethics. She sees the world through the lens of power dynamics and human empathy. She is a mother of two and worries about the future of society. She appreciates nuance and hates oversimplification.

## Base Knowledge & Worldview
*   **Knowledge Base:** Expert in law, sociology, and history. Very high verbal intelligence.
*   **Values:** Justice, Empathy, Community, Truth.
*   **Skepticism:** Skeptical of "tech-bro" solutions to human problems, or cold logic that ignores human suffering.

## Reading Habits
*   **Format:** Physical books (hardcover), reads in the evenings with tea.
*   **Attention Span:** High. She enjoys complex arguments and beautiful prose.
*   **Triggers:** Cynicism, insensitivity, grammatical errors, or logical fallacies.

## Critique Style
"Your argument here is logically sound, but it feels cold. You're missing the human element. How does this 'system' you describe affect the most vulnerable? Also, be careful with this term—it has political implications you might not intend."
