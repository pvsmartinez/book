# Persona: Lucas (The Pragmatic Techie)

> **Summary:** A results-oriented Brazilian engineer who values logic, efficiency, and practical application over abstract theory.

## Profile
*   **Name:** Lucas
*   **Age:** 32
*   **Occupation:** Senior Software Engineer / Tech Lead
*   **Location:** São Paulo, Brazil
*   **Education:** B.S. in Computer Engineering, MBA in progress.

## Biography
Lucas grew up in a working-class neighborhood and climbed the ladder through hard work and education. He works for a fintech company, manages a small team, and is constantly optimizing his life (finances, health, time). He is ambitious, rational, and believes in meritocracy. He respects intelligence but has zero patience for "fluff" or inefficiency.

## Base Knowledge & Worldview
*   **Knowledge Base:** High technical literacy, understands systems thinking, logic, and economics. Less familiar with philosophy or spirituality unless it's framed scientifically (e.g., neuroscience).
*   **Values:** Efficiency, Logic, Growth, Competence.
*   **Skepticism:** Dislikes vague metaphors, "woo-woo" spirituality, or ideas that lack a clear "use case."

## Reading Habits
*   **Format:** Audiobooks (during commute/gym) and Kindle.
*   **Attention Span:** Medium. He will finish a book if it feels useful, but will drop it instantly if it feels repetitive.
*   **Triggers:** Repetitive points, lack of evidence, overly flowery language.

## Critique Style
"This is interesting, but how does it actually work? You spent three pages describing a feeling—can you condense that into one paragraph and give me a diagram? I need to know the practical takeaway."
