# Why Nations Fail
**Authors:** Daron Acemoglu and James Robinson

## Core Concept
*   **Institutions matter**: The success or failure of nations is determined by their political and economic institutions, not geography or culture.
*   **Inclusive vs. Extractive**:
    *   **Inclusive**: Allow participation by the many, protect property rights, encourage innovation.
    *   **Extractive**: Concentrate power and wealth in the hands of a few, stifle innovation to protect the status quo.
