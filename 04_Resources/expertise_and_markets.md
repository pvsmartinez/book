# The Wealth of Nations
*   **Author:** Adam Smith
*   **Relevance:** The origin of "The Invisible Hand." Essential for contrasting the "Ideal Market" vs. the "Real Market" (The Pattern).

# Outliers
*   **Author:** Malcolm Gladwell
*   **Relevance:** Popularized the "10,000 Hour Rule." We need to critique this by adding the "Feedback" component (Anders Ericsson's Deliberate Practice).

# Thinking, Fast and Slow
*   **Author:** Daniel Kahneman
*   **Relevance:** Discusses the "Illusion of Validity" in stock picking and political forecasting. Supports the "Fake Expert" seed.
