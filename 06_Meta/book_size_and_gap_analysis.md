### Word Count Targets
*   **Total Target:** ~60,000 Words.
*   **Page Count:** ~240 Pages.
*   **Reading Time:** ~5 - 6 Hours.
*   **Strategy:** Deeper chapters (more nuance, less "glossing over") and an expanded "Application" section (Part V).

### Breakdown by Chapter Type

| Chapter Type | Count | Avg Words | Avg Pages | Purpose |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Hook (Intro)** | 1 | 1,500 | ~6 | Fast, punchy, sets the stage. |
| **Theory (The Boss)** | 3 | 2,000 | ~8 | Dense, diagram-heavy, explains the math. |
| **Deep Dive (The Story)** | 6 | 2,500 | ~10 | Narrative-driven, immersive, easier reading. |
| **Synthesis (The Bridge)** | 3 | 1,500 | ~6 | Connective tissue, philosophical, reflective. |
| **Conclusion** | 2 | 1,500 | ~6 | Action-oriented, inspiring. |

### Pacing Check
*   **The Rhythm:** The reader gets a "heavy" 8-page theory chapter, followed by two "lighter" 10-page story chapters, then a short 6-page bridge.
*   **Result:** This creates a very digestible flow. The reader never feels bogged down for more than ~15 minutes of reading time.
