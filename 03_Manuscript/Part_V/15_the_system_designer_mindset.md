### 1. The Shift
*   **The Realization:** You are not a victim. You are a node in the network.
*   **The Action:** Stop asking "How do I win?" Start asking "How do I change the rules?"
*   **Key Seeds:** [[the_system_designer_mindset]], [[dont_hate_the_player]].

### 2. The Game Designer Metaphor
*   **The Role:** The Game Designer doesn't hate the players for exploiting the game. They patch the game.
*   **The Application:** Don't hate the billionaire. Fix the tax code. Don't hate the clickbait. Fix the algorithm.

### 3. The Bridge
