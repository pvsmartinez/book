## Specific Edits: Iteration
*   [ ] **Definition:** Iteration = Action + Feedback.
    *   *Clarification:* It's not just "doing it again." It's doing it again *after knowing the result*.
*   [ ] **Examples:**
    *   *Sports:* Kicking a ball. Miss = Negative feedback. Goal = Positive feedback.
    *   *Dog Training:* Sit -> Cookie (Positive). Sit -> No Cookie (Negative).
    *   *School:* Test scores are feedback. Homework is frequent iteration.
*   [ ] **Key Takeaway:** Speed of Iteration x Variance = Speed of Adaptation.
