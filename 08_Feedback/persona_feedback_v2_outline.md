### Conclusion
This **Master Outline** is the "Goldilocks" solution. It successfully integrates the rigor of the **Algorithm** structure with the engagement of the **Narrative** structure. By isolating the "Deep Dives," you allow different personas to engage with the content that resonates most with them while forcing them to learn the underlying framework.
