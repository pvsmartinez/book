## Core Idea: The Invisible Hand as a Judge
*   **The Success:** Works great for small-scale competition (price, creativity, differentiation). It drove the rapid evolution of capitalism.
*   **The Failure:** 
    *   **Time Horizon:** Doesn't handle long-term topics well (climate, infrastructure).
    *   **Monopolies:** When one "winner" takes all, the selection pressure for others disappears.
    *   **Negative Externalities:** Areas where the "Judge" (Profit) ignores the cost to the environment or society.
*   **The Question:** Is the "Invisible Hand" still the right judge for the modern world, or has the environment shifted too much?
